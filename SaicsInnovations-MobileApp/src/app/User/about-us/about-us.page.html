About Us

The South African Institute of cosmetics and slimming (Abbreviated to SAiCS) was established in 1989 and re-branded in 2020 to keep up with modern trends.

Over the years we have grown our extensive range of all natural products under three main categories namely: Slimming, Cosmetics and Lifestyle with new products and ranges being released regularly.

SAiCS as a company can be broken down into 3 main categories being:

  • Manufacturing: We manufacture, bottle and label all our own products.
  • Order management and dispatch: We receive orders from top distributors and dispatch in through outsourced courier companies.
  • Training and Assistance: We provide training, marketing material and support to all our ambassadors.

SAiCS currently has 10 full time employees and over 700 ambassadors spread across southern Africa, Europe and Australasia.
