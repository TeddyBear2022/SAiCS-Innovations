Account

Q.. How do I change my password?


A.. Click on the profile icon, there you will be directed to your profile page. To change your password, click on the "Change password?" link. You will be redirected to the change password page where you can change your current password.



Q.. What is a SAiCS account?


A... SAiCS account is a platform that allows you to connect to one of our trusted ambassadors who are put in place to ease your ordering process.



Q.. How do I view and update my profile details?


A... Click on the profile icon which will direct you to your profile page. There you can view your profile details and choose whether to change or delete your profile.
